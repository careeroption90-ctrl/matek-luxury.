For over a century, Matek Billiards has operated from the same secluded valley in the Swiss Jura. We do not manufacture; we cultivate. Every component of our timepieces is hand-finished by artisans who have dedicated decades to a single discipline.

The "Emerald" signature is more than a color—it is a tribute to the ancient forests that surround our workshop, a symbol of endurance and natural perfection.
